<h3>Advanced Reliability Features: Monte Carlo and Smoke Analysis</h3>

<p>For professional design work, a simulator must do more than just show if a circuit works under ideal conditions; it must predict reliability under stress. Micro-Cap incorporated two highly valuable tools for this:</p>
<p><strong>Monte Carlo Analysis:</strong> As mentioned, this feature tests the circuit across thousands of randomized component value sets (within specified tolerance ranges). The output is a statistical distribution of performance metrics, allowing the engineer to determine the likely manufacturing yield and identify critical components whose variation disproportionately affects the output. This is vital for robust design.</p>
<p><strong>Smoke Analysis:</strong> This unique feature simulates the thermal and power limits of the components. Named colloquially because circuits "let the smoke out" when stressed, Smoke Analysis identifies components that are operating dangerously close to (or exceeding) their maximum power dissipation ratings. This ensures longevity and prevents premature failure of the physical product.</p>
