<p>When forming a government, the negotiating parties must assign ministerial warrants and portfolios. The DPM position is the ultimate bargaining chip for the party that holds the balance of power. <p>If the DPM is the leader of a junior coalition partner, their presence in the second seat provides several crucial benefits to their party:</p>

<ol>

    <li><strong>Succession Assurance:</strong> If the Prime Minister steps down, the DPM is the natural and recognized figure to manage the transition period, minimizing political instability.</li>
</ol>

<p>The arrangement is a delicate balancing act.
