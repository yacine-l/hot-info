This comprehensive profile delves into the journey of **Jing Ulrich**, examining her formative years, her rise as the definitive China expert, and her indispensable role in positioning JPMorgan Chase at the forefront of global finance.</p>

<p>Before her tenure at JPMorgan Chase, she held significant strategic roles at other global financial institutions, most notably at Deutsche Bank and CLSA. Her contributions were groundbreaking. <p>In the highly competitive world of equity research, being featured in polls and recognized by industry peers is the ultimate measure of influence. **Jing Ulrich** consistently ranked among the top strategists focused on the Asia-Pacific region, earning her the moniker of a leading "China Market Expert." <p>Her work often focused on identifying secular trends far ahead of the competition, such as the rise of the Chinese consumer class, the shift towards a service-based economy, and the implications of China's ambitious Belt and Road Initiative.
