<h2>The Birth of a Revolution: Introducing Luna Daily</h2>
<p>In the expansive and often confusing world of beauty and personal care, few brands manage to carve out a genuinely new category. However, since its founding in London in 2019, <strong>Luna Daily</strong> has done precisely that. It is a British cosmetics and feminine hygiene brand that has grown from a niche startup into a prominent voice in the global conversation surrounding women’s health and well-being. Founded by the visionary Katy Cottam, Luna Daily is not merely selling products; it is leading a seismic shift in how women approach their bodies, advocating for what it terms 'whole-body intimate wellness.'</p>

<p>For decades, intimate care was relegated to harsh, clinical products—often packaged with euphemistic language—that were frequently ineffective or, worse, detrimental to the delicate microbial balance of the body. Luna Daily emerged from a profound need to address this gap, challenging the traditional definition of feminine hygiene and normalizing conversations about vulva and vaginal health. The brand’s foundational ethos is simple: every part of a woman’s body deserves gentle, effective, and safe skincare, and that includes the parts historically ignored or treated with suspicion.</p>

<p>Katy Cottam recognized that the skin on and around the intimate area—the vulva—is unique, sensitive, and deserves formulations that prioritize its specific pH and microbiome. By integrating this essential consideration into everyday products, Luna Daily transformed intimate care from a specialized, reactive treatment into proactive, luxurious, and accessible daily body care. This approach has positioned the brand as a key player in the burgeoning $50 billion global wellness industry.</p>

<h2>The Genesis of Luna Daily: Why the Market Needed a Change</h2>
<p>Before <strong>Luna Daily</strong>, the market presented women with two poor options: generic body soaps laden with sulfates, fragrances, and irritants, or highly clinical "feminine wash" products that often perpetuated shame and secrecy. Katy Cottam's inspiration stemmed from recognizing that women were frequently suffering from irritation, dryness, and discomfort caused by everyday body products not formulated for the body’s most sensitive skin.</p>

<p>The standard body care aisle completely overlooked the vulva’s specific biology. The optimal pH level for the skin on the rest of the body is around 5.5, while the vulva requires an even more acidic environment (around 4.5 or lower) to maintain a healthy microbiome. Introducing highly alkaline or perfumed products routinely disrupts this delicate balance, leading to common, irritating issues.</p>

<p>Cottam’s mission was to create a seamless lineup of products—from washes to oils and balms—that could be used safely "everywhere." This meant developing formulations that were dermatologically and gynecologically tested, fragrance-free where necessary, pH-balanced, and focused on supporting the skin barrier and the natural microflora.</p>

<h2>Katy Cottam: The Visionary Behind the Vulva-Safe Revolution</h2>
<p><strong>Katy Cottam</strong> is not just the founder of Luna Daily; she is the driving force behind the brand’s mission of destigmatization. Her background in the beauty and retail sector provided her with the commercial acumen needed to scale a business, but her personal passion for health and challenging industry norms fueled its unique direction.</p>

<p>Cottam’s own experiences, combined with extensive research into the lack of education surrounding women's bodies, confirmed her belief that the solution lay in transparency and science-backed ingredients. She often emphasizes that talking about vulva health should be as normal as discussing skincare for the face. Under her leadership, Luna Daily quickly became synonymous with 'vulva-safe skincare,' a term that encapsulates their commitment to formulations that support, rather than strip, the natural protective barrier.</p>
<p>Her commitment to education extends beyond product development. Cottam ensured that Luna Daily actively engages in conversation about menopause, pregnancy, post-partum changes, and common intimate health issues, fostering a community that feels safe and informed.</p>

<h2>Beyond Feminine Hygiene: Defining 'The Whole Body Intimate Wellness' Category</h2>
<p>The key differentiator for <strong>Luna Daily</strong> lies in its creation of the ‘whole-body intimate wellness’ category. This philosophy moves away from the reactive, problem-solving narrative of traditional feminine hygiene and shifts towards a proactive, holistic self-care ritual. The brand’s products are designed to be used on the entire body, from head to toe, with the crucial guarantee that they are safe enough for the most sensitive areas.</p>

<p>This approach addresses a fundamental user dilemma: why should women need two separate sets of products—one for the body, and one for the vulva—when they can use one product line that protects both? By creating products that are universally gentle and formulated to match the vulva's pH, Luna Daily has simplified the beauty routine while elevating the standard of care.</p>

<p>Crucially, all Luna Daily products are:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>pH-Balanced:</strong> Specifically formulated to support the optimal, slightly acidic pH of the vulva.</li>
    <li><strong>Microbiome-Friendly:</strong> Often fortified with prebiotics and/or postbiotics to nurture beneficial bacteria.</li>
    <li><strong>Free from Harsh Irritants:</strong> Excluding sulfates, parabens, strong fragrances, and glycerin (which can be problematic for certain intimate areas).</li>
    <li><strong>Hormone-Safe:</strong> Tested to ensure they do not interfere with hormonal cycles or treatments.</li>
</ul>

<p>This rigorous commitment to safety and efficacy is what gives customers the confidence to use the products on their whole body without compromise.</p>

<h2>The Science of Safety: Understanding the Luna Daily Formulation</h2>
<p>The effectiveness of <strong>Luna Daily</strong> products is rooted in their intelligent ingredient profile. The brand champions the use of prebiotics, postbiotics, and specialized natural ingredients designed to calm irritation, lock in moisture, and maintain the skin’s integrity.</p>

<p>The skin's microbiome—the community of microorganisms living on the surface—is critical for defense against pathogens and irritation. In the intimate area, this microbiome is particularly fragile. Luna Daily's formulations prioritize maintaining the dominance of beneficial bacteria, primarily through the inclusion of the brand's proprietary blend, LACTO-B. This focus on the microbiome differentiates their offerings from standard body care products that can often strip or imbalance the skin’s natural defenses.</p>

<p>Furthermore, the brand’s dedication to testing is paramount. Every product undergoes rigorous testing by independent dermatologists and gynecologists, ensuring they meet the highest standards for vulva-safe skincare. This meticulous approach is vital in fostering trust among consumers who have long been promised solutions that often failed to deliver.</p>

<h2>Building a Community: Education and Empowerment</h2>
<p>Luna Daily understands that true change requires more than just excellent products; it requires education and community. <strong>Katy Cottam</strong> built the brand with a strong educational component aimed at normalizing vulva literacy. Historically, there has been a profound lack of accurate information about women’s bodies, leading to confusion, discomfort, and delayed diagnosis of health issues.</p>

<p>Through its digital platforms, partnerships with health professionals, and active customer engagement, Luna Daily promotes open dialogue about common yet often taboo subjects, such as vaginal discharge, hormonal fluctuations, perimenopause, and sex-related skin discomfort. By empowering women with clear, factual information, the brand transforms the act of buying intimate care into an act of self-respect and wellness advocacy.</p>

<p>This commitment to breaking down taboos has resonated deeply with modern consumers who prioritize transparency and authenticity. The brand’s aesthetic—minimalist, chic, and luxurious—intentionally steers clear of the clinical or overly "feminine" packaging that characterized its predecessors, further integrating these products into the premium self-care routines they deserve.</p>
